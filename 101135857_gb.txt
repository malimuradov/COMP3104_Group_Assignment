                                                      George Brown College

George Brown College, is located in the province of Ontario within Canada's largest city Toronto. What makes George Brown College (GBC) different from other colleges is that it's located in the heart of downtown next to Bay Street and the nearby government buildings give students access to future employers. GBC offers three campuses that host a variety of full-time and part-time programs throughout the year. They are Casa Loma campus located at 160 Kendal Avenue, St. James campus, located at 200 King Street East and Waterfront Campus located at 51 Dockside Drive. With its many programs taught by great professional professors, GBC is the right choice for future college students.
